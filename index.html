SKS-MATHS ACADEMY

In today’s competitive world, eminent educationalists are compelled to move to higher levels performance benchmarks. It is increasingly not enough for esteemed institutions to just have a 100% pass results in the board exams as a target. Success in competitive exams such as Olympiads, NTSE,IIT JEE, etc……. is the new parameter of success. Pressure to perform at the board exams, compelled with the acute need to excel in competitive exams is creating high levels of stress in students and parents. This has an adverse impact on the overall personality of the student. Hence it is important that the institutions look at ways to reduce this stress and enable holistic personality development of the students.

SKS-MATHS, an initiative by pioneers in the field of education over the past 20 years, made humble yet significant contributions to the competitive examinations training space. Keeping the need of hour in mind, SKS-MATHS has evolved many programmes and proposals that are beneficial to all stake holders: Teachers, students, parents and SKS-MATHS.

SKS-MATHS programmes are aimed at achieving and setting such performance benchmarks that are unassailable for other institutions and offering a stress free but highly result oriented education system for students.

SKS-MATHS integrated programmes involve complete participation by SKS-MATHS professionals in training students, all aspects of succeeding in competitive exams. In addition they will also imbibe in the students, the science of success in various competitive exams. The ultimate aim would be jointly create success on all parameters.

Programs Offered by Us

Mathematics

SKS-MATHS programs are designed to help students in overall development.

Programs are designed irrespective of Boards they come from like C.B.S.E, ICSE or STATE BOARD.

Syllabus will be taught from basic level to advance level that helps students to take various entrance exams. Overall idea is to send them into best Engineering colleges of India

Overall this programs will help students to make them future ready.

TWO-YEAR IIT-JEE INTEGRATED PROGRAM FOR 10TH PASSED STUDENTS

This Program is designed for 10th Passed students in such a way that the entire syllabus is taught from basic level to Advance level.Which prepares students not only for MAINS & ADVANCE But for other entrances also like BITSAT,KVPY,OLYMPIAD,IISC etc.

ONE-YEAR IIT-JEE INTEGRATED PROGRAM FOR 11TH PASSED STUDENTS

This Program is designed for 11th Passed students. Which prepares students for IIT-JEE along with intermediate board.

ONE-YEAR IIT-JEE LONG TERM PROGRAM FOR 12TH PASSED STUDENTS

This Program is designed for 12th Passed students & Repeaters. Where the entire syllabus of JEE is taught. Students has go through a rigorous training program for a period of one year.
